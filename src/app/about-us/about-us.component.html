About Us

Shri gayatri Education is a non-profitable, Non-Government Organization (NGO), established in 2019 under Society Registration Act 1860 void registration number GBN/10274/2018-19 that aims to underprivileged people of the society, to empower them in respect of education, health, skill development, employment etc., who are deprived from accessing the main stream resources.

We serve for national skill development schemes from state/central Govt. for youth, divayangs etc., handicraft training programs for women/girls, Swachh Bharat mission, Beti Bachao Beti Padao, environmental sustainability and more others which is deemed for welfare of the society.

Our Vision
